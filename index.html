How It Works

1. Reach Out

Call, text, or email with your project details. I may request photos or schedule a quick on-site visit to fully understand the goal.

2. Plan & Estimate

We discuss your expectations, requirements, and any specifics. I do additional research if needed and give you a clear estimate.

3.Schedule the Work

Once everything is aligned, we pick a date and time that works for you. I’ll send a confirmation message the day of.

4. The Work

I show up on time, walk you through the plan, and get to work — cleanly and professionally. If a project spans multiple days, I explain what’s been completed and what’s next.

5. Completion & Follow-Up

When the job is done, I make sure everything is cleaned up and you’re fully satisfied.
